Activities

Bruno's favorite thing to do is curl up in the sun, or assume his frog position, and take a nice nap in the sunlight, usually accompanied with his favorite person Morgan. He's always ecstatic to be taken on an adventure whether its an off leash walk in the woods, getting a trip to the dog park to make new friends, a plain old walk to sniff new things, or just playing fetch in the backyard with his siblings.

Adoption Story

Bruno has a very simple, but sweet adoption story. He is a rescue from Georgia that was transported from Georgia to Virginia when he was around 9-12 months old. His gotcha day is in May, he was a sight on seen adoption, although we did have 2 weeks to decide if he was a keeper, which we did not need to decide he was meant to be a part of the family. We gave him a June birthday and celebrated his first birthday a month after getting him. We showered him with love and treats, he put on some weight, learned some tricks, and 4 years later, he' as happy as can be.
